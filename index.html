Meet Jimmy

Full Professional Biography

JG

James Grigo

Playing Career

James competed on the 1995 ITF Tour, representing his skills on the world stage in the International Tennis Federation World Championship. His competitive edge and deep understanding of the game have shaped his approach to coaching, making him a sought-after instructor for players of all levels.

Coaching Experience

James began his coaching journey at just 16 in The Hamptons, NY, working with top clubs and private facilities, including:

  • East Hamptons Indoors Tennis
  • West Hamptons Tennis & Sports
  • Hamptons Athletic Club, now SportTime
  • Exclusive Private Clubs & Courts

From the age of 25 to 35, he became a renowned tennis instructor in California, coaching at Tustin Hills Country Club, also known as Tustin Hills Racquet Club.

Since the age of 36, James has continued his success as a leading tennis coach in Florida, working with Club Med, Team Tennis, Langford Park, Sailfish Park, and private tennis clubs and courts.

Starting 2025, James will be splitting his time coaching in Florida during the winter and The Hamptons during the summer.

Central Legacy

A tradition built on craftsmanship and excellence

Central Tennis is part of a multi-generational family legacy rooted in Southampton, New York. The Grigo family has spent decades building a reputation for precision, discipline, and quality through their work in both athletics and craftsmanship.

From Central Kitchens to Central Tennis, the same core values remain: attention to detail, commitment to excellence, and long-term relationships. This foundation carries over onto the court, where every lesson reflects a tradition of professionalism and pride.

Two traditions. One family. One standard of excellence.
